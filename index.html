About us

MEGATECH INDUSTRIAL SUPPLIES & SERVICES offers access to a broad range of products and services to local and international markets. We pride ourselves on being able to supply procurement needs and undertake associated services with client requirements in diverse sectors such as Agricultural Industry, Construction, Energy (Oil & Gas, Water & Electricity), and Pharmaceutical Industry.

History

MEGATECH INDUSTRIAL SUPPLIES & SERVICES began its business last March 2017. The slowing price of oil and gas in the world market since last quarter of 2014 up to 2017, which has resulted in the global recession that affects many countries around the world, caused retrenchment of overseas workers (OFW) hence prompted the birth of this corporation by seven (7) incorporators whom generally are composed of professional OFWs, a businessman and a doctor of veterinary medicine. The founding President & CEO Marlon V. Malana made the best efforts and instrumental for the formation of the company consisting incorporators with exceptional expertise in the various fields of procurement, contracts, construction, power & industrial engineering, material control/management and planning, and business enterprise.

We, at MEGATECH, have over long years of experience and knowledge both local overseas to share with our valued customers or clients.

Mission

We pride ourselves on prompt response to client’s inquiries, preparation of focused and precise techno-commercial offers, and timely execution of orders. We only provide the best quality products combined with timely after-sales support and service.

Vision

We are committed to foster long-lasting relationships by offering high-quality, innovative equipment, materials, spare parts, components and services for a variety of industries. We strive to maintain the trust and confidence of all the clients that we do business with.
